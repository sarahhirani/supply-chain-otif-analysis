# OTIF Service Performance Analysis
**Supply Chain Analytics Project | Fulfillment Operations**

## Overview
This project analyzes fulfillment service performance using the OTIF (On-Time-In-Full) metric to identify operational constraints and improvement opportunities.

OTIF is a critical KPI used across retail, CPG, and manufacturing to measure delivery reliability and customer service performance.

---

## Objective
Evaluate fulfillment reliability and identify the operational drivers impacting service levels, then model improvement scenarios to understand what changes are required to approach a 95% OTIF target.

---

## Tools & Methods
- Tableau (data visualization & dashboards)
- Excel (data preparation & KPI logic)
- OTIF KPI framework
- Pareto analysis
- Scenario modeling

---

## Key Performance Metrics
**OTIF (On-Time-In-Full)**  
Measures whether orders are delivered on time and in full.

**Supporting metrics:**
- Fill rate
- Late shipment rate
- Delay days
- SKU-level failure contribution

---

## Dashboard Components

### Executive OTIF Overview
Tracks service performance vs the 95% target.

### Late Shipments by Carrier
Evaluates whether transportation providers are driving delays.

### Warehouse Performance Analysis
Identifies fulfillment throughput variability across distribution centers.

### SKU Failure Pareto
Highlights high-impact products driving service failures.

### OTIF Improvement Simulator
Interactive model demonstrating how operational improvements can improve service levels.

---

## Key Insights

- OTIF performance was significantly below the 95% service target.
- Partial shipments and warehouse delays were the primary drivers of failure.
- A small subset of SKUs generated a disproportionate share of service failures.
- Carrier performance was not the primary constraint.
- Service reliability is driven by system performance rather than a single operational variable.

---

## Improvement Scenario Modeling

Scenario modeling indicates service performance can improve significantly through targeted operational changes:

| Scenario | Projected OTIF |
|----------|---------------|
| Current Performance | 26.5% |
| Improve Fill Rate | ~58% |
| Reduce Warehouse Delays | ~72% |
| Improve Top SKU Availability | ~84% |
| Combined Improvements | ~90–95% |
